Resins

Origin

Our natural resins are mainly produced from Pinus ELLIOTTI specie. Basically from South American extractors. As for the Hydrocarbon Resins we are a sole distributor of one of the major EXXON Mobile spin-offs operations from China.

Applications

We supply a wide variety of modified and esterified rosins according to the final application of our clients. Some of the industries we act are the Adhesives, Cleaning Products, Rubber, Chewing Gum and Sweets, Post Harvest industries, Paints and Road Markings.

Waxes

Origin

Our Natural waxes range from Palm Trees waxes from South American and Asian origin. Synthetic waxes group are generally sourced from High end Petrochemical enterprises both in Americas and Asia Pacific.

Applications

Cosmetics, Food, Pharmaceutical, Cleaning and candle making are general applications of Natural Waxes supplied by us. While our synthetic waxes are mostly focused into HMA, Paints, Candle Making, Additives for Rubber and Plastics.
